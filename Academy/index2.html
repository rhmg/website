Please be welcome to the Rock Hard Academy.



Are you a beginner level drummer looking to increase your knowledge and skill for drumming? Or maybe a casual drummer looking to pick up tips to help you to progress to a better ability level?

We are here to help.

Our drum lessons are provided by experienced Rock / Funk / Soul / Blues drummers with many years of session work and gigging behind them.

Sessions last for 45 minutes, and cost £20. Uniquely we are based in Rock Hard Studios, the Fylde's newest, biggest and best professional recording and rehearsal facility.

Your session will take place in the 400 square foot live room (click for a picture). This means that you will benefit from a clean, spacious and airy custom built facility.

You will also be recorded every 10 sessions, so you have a record of your progress. This is inclusive in the price, and will be to a backing track of your choice, which will be central to each session.

For more information, and to book your first session, please contact us on 01253 749761.
